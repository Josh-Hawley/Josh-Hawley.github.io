Planets simulation

"It's not a ghost, it's gravity!" [1]

A simple simulation of planets orbiting the sun (and each other). Play around with the sliders to change strength of gravity 'G' and the number of planets. You can also drag the Sun around (if you are on a desktop).

G = 600
Number of planets = 2
Trail length = 2
