What's on this website?

This website contains all the interesting Javascript projects I have made/working on. At the moment this consists of variations of simulating many particles that have simple rules for interacting with each other, which results in interesting complex behaviour. There's a gravitaional example below, and if you click the tabs at the top, you'll find other simulations, and the nerual tube sim is based on my PhD work.

"It's not a ghost, it's gravity." [1]

The particles or planets below are programmed to follow Newton's law of universal gravitation [2], meaning each particle attracts every other particle with a force dependent on the distance between them as well as the product of both their masses.

This simple interaction results in chaotic motion, not solvable without simulations like this! Play around with the sliders below, or drag the sun around (big yellow blob).

G = 600
